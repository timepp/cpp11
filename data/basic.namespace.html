[basic.namespace]7.3 Namespaces

1A namespace is an optionally-named declarative region. The name of a namespace can be used to access entities declared in that namespace; that is, the members of the namespace. Unlike other declarative regions, the definition of a namespace can be split over several parts of one or more translation units.

2The outermost declarative region of a translation unit is a namespace; see 3.3.6.
