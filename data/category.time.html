[category.time]22.4.5 The time category

1Templates time_get<charT,InputIterator> and time_put<charT,OutputIterator> provide date and time formatting and parsing. All specifications of member functions for time_put and time_get in the subclauses of 22.4.5 only apply to the specializations required in Tables 81 and 782 (22.3.1.1.1). Their members use their ios_base&, ios_base::iostate&, and fill arguments as described in (22.4), and the ctype<> facet, to determine formatting details.
