[filebuf.assign]27.9.1.3 Assign and swap

basic_filebuf& operator=(basic_filebuf&& rhs);

1Effects: Calls this->close() then move assigns from rhs. After the move assignment *this has the observable state it would have had if it had been move constructed from rhs (see 27.9.1.2).

2Returns: *this.

void swap(basic_filebuf& rhs);

3Effects: Exchanges the state of *this and rhs.

template <class charT, class traits> void swap(basic_filebuf<charT, traits>& x, basic_filebuf<charT, traits>& y);

4Effects: x.swap(y).
