[istream.iterator.cons]24.6.1.1 istream_iterator constructors and destructor

see below istream_iterator();

1Effects: Constructs the end-of-stream iterator. If T is a literal type, then this constructor shall be a constexpr constructor.

2Postconditions: in_stream == 0.

istream_iterator(istream_type& s);

3Effects: Initializes in_stream with &s. value may be initialized during construction or the first time it is referenced.

4Postconditions: in_stream == &s.

istream_iterator(const istream_iterator& x) = default;

5Effects: Constructs a copy of x. If T is a literal type, then this constructor shall be a trivial copy constructor.

6Postconditions: in_stream == x.in_stream.

~istream_iterator() = default;

7Effects: The iterator is destroyed. If T is a literal type, then this destructor shall be a trivial destructor.
