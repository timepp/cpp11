[istreambuf.iterator.cons]24.6.3.2 istreambuf_iterator constructors

constexpr istreambuf_iterator() noexcept;

1Effects: Constructs the end-of-stream iterator.

istreambuf_iterator(basic_istream<charT, traits>& s) noexcept; istreambuf_iterator(basic_streambuf<charT, traits>* s) noexcept;

2Effects: Constructs an istreambuf_iterator<> that uses the basic_streambuf<> object *(s.rdbuf()), or *s, respectively. Constructs an end-of-stream iterator if s.rdbuf() is null.

istreambuf_iterator(const proxy& p) noexcept;

3Effects: Constructs a istreambuf_iterator<> that uses the basic_streambuf<> object pointed to by the proxy object’s constructor argument p.
