[make.heap]25.4.6.3 make_heap 

template<class RandomAccessIterator>
  void make_heap(RandomAccessIterator first, RandomAccessIterator last);

template<class RandomAccessIterator, class Compare>
  void make_heap(RandomAccessIterator first, RandomAccessIterator last,
                 Compare comp);

1Effects: Constructs a heap out of the range [first, last).

2Requires: The type of *first shall satisfy the MoveConstructible requirements (Table 20) and the the MoveAssignable requirements (Table 22).

3Complexity: At most 3 * (last - first) comparisons.
