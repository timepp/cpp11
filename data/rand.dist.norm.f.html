[rand.dist.norm.f]26.5.8.5.5 Class template fisher_f_distribution

1A fisher_f_distribution random number distribution produces random numbers x ≥ 0 distributed according to the probability density function p(x | m, n) = Γ((m + n)/2)/Γ(m/2)Γ(n/2) ⋅ (m/n)m/2 ⋅ x(m/2)−1 ⋅ (1+mx/n)−(m+n)/2.
