[re.results.swap]28.10.7 match_results swap

void swap(match_results& that);

1Effects: Swaps the contents of the two sequences.

2Postconditions: *this contains the sequence of matched sub-expressions that were in that, that contains the sequence of matched sub-expressions that were in *this.

3Complexity: constant time.

template <class BidirectionalIterator, class Allocator> void swap(match_results<BidirectionalIterator, Allocator>& m1, match_results<BidirectionalIterator, Allocator>& m2);

4Effects: m1.swap(m2).
