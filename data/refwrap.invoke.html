[refwrap.invoke]20.8.3.4 reference_wrapper invocation

template <class... ArgTypes> typename result_of<T&(ArgTypes&&... )>::type operator()(ArgTypes&&... args) const;

1Returns: INVOKE(get(), std::forward<ArgTypes>(args)...). (20.8.2)

2Remarks: operator() is described for exposition only. Implementations are not required to provide an actual reference_wrapper::operator(). Implementations are permitted to support reference_wrapper function invocation through multiple overloaded operators or through other means.
