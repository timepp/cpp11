[reverse.iter.requirements]24.5.1.2 reverse_iterator requirements

1The template parameter Iterator shall meet all the requirements of a Bidirectional Iterator (24.2.6).

2Additionally, Iterator shall meet the requirements of a Random Access Iterator (24.2.7) if any of the members operator+ (24.5.1.3.8), operator- (24.5.1.3.10), operator+= (24.5.1.3.9), operator-= (24.5.1.3.11), operator[] (24.5.1.3.12), or the global operators operator< (24.5.1.3.14), operator> (24.5.1.3.16), operator<= (24.5.1.3.18), operator>= (24.5.1.3.17), operator- (24.5.1.3.19) or operator+ (24.5.1.3.20), is referenced in a way that requires instantiation (14.7.1).
