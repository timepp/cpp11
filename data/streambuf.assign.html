[streambuf.protected]27.6.3.3 basic_streambuf protected member functions

[streambuf.assign]27.6.3.3.1 Assignment
basic_streambuf& operator=(const basic_streambuf& rhs);

1Effects: Assigns the data members of rhs to *this.

2Postconditions:

  • eback() == rhs.eback()
  • gptr() == rhs.gptr()
  • egptr() == rhs.egptr()
  • pbase() == rhs.pbase()
  • pptr() == rhs.pptr()
  • epptr() == rhs.epptr()
  • getloc() == rhs.getloc()

3Returns: *this.

void swap(basic_streambuf& rhs);

4Effects: Swaps the data members of rhs and *this.
