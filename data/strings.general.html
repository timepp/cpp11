[strings]21 Strings library

[strings.general]21.1 General

1This Clause describes components for manipulating sequences of any non-array POD (3.9) type. In this Clause such types are called char-like types, and objects of char-like types are called char-like objects or simply characters.

2The following subclauses describe a character traits class, a string class, and null-terminated sequence utilities, as summarized in Table 61.
